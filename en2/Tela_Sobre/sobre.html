ABOUT THE PROJECT

HackInSDN is a project focused on empowering cybersecurity professionals through a robust testbed environment. It offers tools such as anomaly detection with AI, attack containment, threat simulation, and network monitoring. It acts as a hands-on laboratory, preparing specialists for real-world scenarios.

With support from RNP, it combines programmable networks and intrusion detection systems like Suricata, along with traffic mirroring and threat intelligence modules.

Hackers do Bem 2023

HackInSDN was selected in the 2023 Hackers do Bem Call, standing out as an innovative project in cybersecurity. It contributes to professional training by offering advanced tools and simulating real attacks in a safe environment. The project promotes collaboration among institutions and is constantly evolving, with open support to the community for continuous improvements.
